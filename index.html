Privacy Policy for Scalr

Last updated: 7 June 2026

Scalr ("the app") is a calculator application designed to run entirely on your device. This page explains how the app handles information.

Data collection

Scalr does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, or analytics. The app does not require an account, an internet connection, or any sign-in, and it does not contain advertising.

Local storage

Anything you create within the app — such as calculation history, saved workspaces, custom variables, and settings — is stored only on your device using standard local storage. This information is never transmitted anywhere and stays fully under your control; you can clear it at any time from within the app, or by clearing the app's storage from your device's system settings.

Permissions

Scalr requests the following device permission:

  • Vibration — used to provide haptic feedback (a light buzz when you tap certain buttons or complete an action). This can be turned off from within the app's settings at any time.

Third parties

Scalr does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No information is shared with any third party, because no information ever leaves your device.

Children's privacy

Scalr does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect information from anyone at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is ever updated, the revised version will be posted at this same address with an updated "Last updated" date.
